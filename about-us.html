ABOUT JACKSON HOLDINGS

What started out as a one truck moving business in the Germantown section of Philadelphia in 1998 when Chavone Jackson was just 19-years-old has now expanded to include five thriving businesses: Elvis Dirt Cheap Moving (EDCM), Ming Self Storage, Myrtle Jean Lofts, By Your Side Home Care and M & E Management.

Elvis Dirt Cheap Moving has been servicing the Philadelphia region and long distance clients for more than two decades. Ming Self Storage was founded in 2005 and has expanded to three locations: Germantown, Northern Liberties and Olney. In 2019 Jackson Holdings became a partner in By Your Side Home Care and in the same year, Myrtle Jean Lofts, a co-working space and salon suites was added to the company roster of enterprises. M & E Management, established in 2008, is a commercial & residential property management company.

Our mission is rooted in the belief that entrepreneurship can be a catalyst for social and economic progress. By nurturing and empowering entrepreneurs, we aim to create a ripple effect that uplifts our community.
